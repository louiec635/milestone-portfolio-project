about me

A website is a necessity and I wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs. That curiosity then opened a door that

could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
